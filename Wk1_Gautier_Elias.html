  • Elias

    • Graduated from the Central High Arts School in Santurce Puerto Rico in 1998, Elias likes to get his ideas straight from his mind and do his best in creating the job. Here in his girl at the beach, the paint has a funny story. Elias painted the girl just with a nice small bikini and when he brought the art to his teacher, she said it was a wonderful job, but too strong for a high school kid so she ask Elias to paint the skirt on the girl. Funny thing is that the girl that Elias painted from his mind looks exactly like his wife.. maybe it was destiny.

  • Carelis

    • Like her brother, Carelis also graduated from Central High Arts School and took herself through the art world in a special way. This paint here by Carelis represented Puerto Rico for a year at the U.S. congress as part of an art Expo. Carelis painted the piece overnight just being responsible with her duties and deliver her job. Amazing that just a simple paint of a beach will represent the island in such an important place.
